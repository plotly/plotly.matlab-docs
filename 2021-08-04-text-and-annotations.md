## Adding Text to Plots with the <code>text</code> Function


```{matlab}
x = -pi:pi/10:pi;
y = sin(x);
figure('Name', 'Sample graph'), plot(x, y, '--rs');

for i=8:size(x,2)-8
text(x(i), y(i), 'Text');
end

fig2plotly(gcf);
```
